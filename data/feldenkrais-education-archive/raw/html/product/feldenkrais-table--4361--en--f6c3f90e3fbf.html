Description

Habys brand table, specifically designed for the practice of the Feldenkrais Method in Functional Integration, created in collaboration with therapists and trainers.

Thanks to its three pairs of feet and transverse stabilization bars, this table stands out for its exceptional stability. Its surface of 80 x 192 cm allows for a comfortable practice and ensures the ease and safety of both the practitioner and the student.

The combination of a firmer bottom layer made of durable foam and a softer top layer offers both the necessary support and a sense of comfort that optimizes relaxation.

Technical specifications:

Length: 192 cm

Width: 80 cm

Height (adjustable): 44-60 cm

Dimensions after the beach: 97 x 83 x 19,5 cm

Weight (+/- 1 kg): 14,6 kg

Dynamic resistance*: 450 kg

Static resistance**: 1400 kg

Material: Wood

Foam thickness: 2.5 cm

Brand: Habys

• Dynamic resistance – measurement made with a uniformly distributed dynamic load.

** Static resistance – measurement made with a uniformly distributed fixed static load.
